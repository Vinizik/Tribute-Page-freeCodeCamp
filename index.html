Anthony Edward Stark

Also known as Tony Stark, the man who saved the universe

Here's a time line of Tony Stark life:

  • 1970 - Tony Stark Is Born (Manhattan)
  • 1983 - Tony Hacks Into The Pentagon On A Dare From Friends
  • 1987 - Tony Graduates Summa Cum Laude From MIT
  • 1991 - Tony Loses His Parents To An Alleged Car Crash
  • 1992 - Tony Takes Over Stark Industries
  • 1999 - Tony Blows Off Several Genius Scientists To Hook Up With Maya Hansen
  • 2009 - Tony Is Taken Prisoner After Demonstrating The Jericho Prototype, Eventually Escaping After Creating A Makeshift Battle Suit, 1 Month Later Tony Fights His Business Mentor To Protect His Arc Reactor Technology
  • 2010 - Tony Prevents A Drone Attack At The Stark Expo Organized By The Son Of One Of Howard Stark's Rivals
  • 2012 - Tony Unites The Avengers To Stop Loki's Chitauri Incursion Of Earth And Stops Aldrich Killian From Assassinating The President
  • 2015 - Tony Creates Ultron In A Bid To Protect The World From Future Onslaughts, And This Results In The Battle Of Sokovia
  • 2016 - Tony Fights Captain America And His Allies Over Surrendering Bucky Barnes And The Signing Of The Sokovia Accords
  • 2017 - Tony Mentors Peter Parker, Saves A Bunch Of People On The Staten Island Ferry, And Gets Engaged To Pepper Potts
  • 2018 - Tony And The Avengers Fail To Stop Thanos From Uniting The Infinity Stones, After that, Tony Returns To Earth After Captain Marvel Rescues Him
  • 2019 - Tony Becomes A Father After Pepper Gives Birth To Morgan Stark
  • 2023 - Tony Solves Time Travel, Helps Orchestrate The Infinity Stone Time Heist And Snaps Thanos Out Of Existence, Perishing From The Strain On His Body
